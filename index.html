Cub's Mission

We are a student-run management consulting organization that provides innovative and comprehensive strategies primarily to nonprofits, startups, and small businesses in our community.

Who Are We?

At Cub, we are a tight-knit community of passionate students from diverse majors who strive to expand the horizons and possibilities of what our students can do both in the business consulting world and in our community. We provide hands-on experience to support our members in their journey toward being impactful changemakers in their communities and potential careers in the consulting industry.

What Do We Offer?

Our organization specializes in providing tailored solutions to meet the diverse needs of our clients. From strategic planning and marketing to impactful design, we ensure every organization we work with thrives and achieves its goals.
