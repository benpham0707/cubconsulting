Cub's Mission

Cub Consulting at Berkeley empowers the local community by providing pro-bono consulting services to startups, small businesses, and student organizations. We are committed to driving impact, fostering innovation, and developing UC Berkeley students through meaningful collaborations and community engagement.

Who Are We?

We are a team of passionate UC Berkeley students dedicated to enriching organizations in our community. Through our pro-bono consulting services, we provide tailored solutions for startups, small businesses, and student groups to help them thrive and maximize their impact.

What Do We Offer?

Our organization specializes in providing tailored solutions to meet the diverse needs of our clients. From strategic planning and marketing to impactful design, we ensure every organization we work with thrives and achieves its goals.
