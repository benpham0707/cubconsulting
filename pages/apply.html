JOIN US

Be apart of the Cub Consulting Family!

WE WANT YOU TO JOIN!

We encourage applications from students who are passionate about consulting, regardless of their major. We are looking for individuals who have delved deeply into their passions, showcase strong problem-solving skills, think creatively when designing solutions, and give back to their communities. Cub Consulting recruits new consultants on a semesterly basis, and we hope to see you apply soon!
